A little about me

I started out as the kid who ran the design department and co-founded a science club, so my instinct has always been to make something people actually want to use, then make it work behind the scenes.

These days that means writing backend services that handle tens of thousands of requests a day, shaving API latency from hundreds of milliseconds down to double digits, and still sweating the 8 px of padding on a dashboard. I've shipped full-stack features, RPA automation, and a sentiment-analysis pipeline that judges picked as top-10%.

Outside the editor I'm usually in Unity or Unreal, swimming, or drawing something. I think the people who build the best software are the ones who make other things too.
